People-watchers' delight
Rating: 4
Date: October 15, 2016
Despite the red-and-white linoleum feel of its interior, Gott's Roadside is not really a "roadside" diner. On one side is the Embarcadero, with its roaring traffic flow, and the historic Ferry Building and ferry docks on the other. Their outdoor seating is perfect for people-watching and "train-spotting" the old trolleys lumbering by.Decent food & prices, cold beer. I could've used a little more "fish" and a little less "chips" in my Fish & Chips, but the fish was tasty. My pal had the ahi and devoured it quickly. Our outdoor table was wobbly & sticky. Still, Gott's Roadside is a fun dining experience.
