Go for breakfast...you won't regret it!
Rating: 5
Date: February 26, 2017
Picture this: My last day in San Francisco after a brutal work week, it is pouring down rain, and I just need a good thing to happen in the last hours of my trip. I need to go home -- I'm tired and my flight is delayed and I need a good breakfast and coffee. So I head out of my hotel on Nob Hill, and descend the steep roadway to Roxanne Cafe. As I get to the corner where Roxanne Cafe is located, I see the warm lights and it beckons me in. So begins my last meal -- and best in this recent trip to San Francisco. Roxanne Cafe is perfect -- intimate with good seating, good coffee and excellent, pleasant service. I had a seat by the windows so I could watch the rain and the people bustling down the street. I had for breakfast the "Corned Beef Sunrise" - Corned beef hash with country potatoes, 2 poached eggs covered in hollandaise sauce and toast. I like to think I'm an expert in poached eggs. I make them a lot at home. The ones at Roxanne Cafe were PERFECT. Not close. PERFECT. The hash was more a slice of corn beef seasoned and caramelized on the grill. SO GOOD. The coffee was hot and good, and while not strong, it was delicious and I had several cups while watching the rain. I just thought this place was a perfect balance of ambiance, delicious food, hot coffee and good staff. Totally solid. I'd definitely come again when I'm in San Francisco.
